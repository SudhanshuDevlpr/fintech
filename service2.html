Check Verification with Checkfac

Secure, accurate, and trusted validation

Before any payment is processed, verifying account and transaction details is critical. Checkfac’s advanced check verification system is designed to protect both merchants and payers by validating information in real time — reducing fraud, chargebacks, and costly errors.

With Checkfac, every transaction request is screened through intelligent verification layers: account validation, routing checks, negative databases, historical patterns, and risk scoring. This ensures that funds are only initiated from legitimate, active accounts authorized by the true owner.

Merchants gain confidence accepting high-value and recurring payments, while customers enjoy a smooth, interruption-free experience. No guesswork, no manual validation — Checkfac automates the verification process so your business can focus on growth, not risk management.

Whether you handle subscriptions, invoicing, payouts, or cross-border collections, Checkfac Check Verification adds an essential security layer that keeps your transactions compliant, traceable, and trustworthy from the very first step.
